# Fear *Spell 1*   
[emotion](emotion.md)  [enchantment](enchantment.md)  [fear](3)%20Rules/traits/fear.md)  [mental](mental.md)

- **Cast** `PF2:2` somatic, verbal
- **Range**: 30 foot
- **Targets**: one creature
- **Saving Throw**: Will
- **Duration**: varies

You plant fear in the target; it must attempt a Will save.

> [!success-degree] 
> - **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
> - **Success** The target is [frightened](conditions.md#Frightened 1).
> - **Failure** The target is [frightened](conditions.md#Frightened 2).
> - **Critical Failure** The target is [frightened](conditions.md#Frightened 3) and feeing for 1 round.
