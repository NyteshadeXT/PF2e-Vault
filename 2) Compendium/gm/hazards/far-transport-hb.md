# Far Transport *Hazard 15*  
[complex](rules/traits/complex.md "Complex Hazard Trait")  [divination](rules/traits/divination.md "Divination School Trait")  [magical](rules/traits/magical.md "Magical Item Trait")  [teleportation](rules/traits/teleportation.md "Teleportation Item Trait")  [trap](rules/traits/trap.md "Trap Hazard Trait")  

- **Complexity** Complex
- **Stealth** +37  (master) to notice faint psychic resonance emanating from the lever and the surrounding walls.

A hidden psychic relay bound to the secret door reacts violently to intruders, shunting victims through a dimensional conduit into the torture chamber.

- **Disable** DC 38 [[skills#Thievery|Thievery]] (master) to unlock the secret door without touching the lever. [[skills#Arcana|Arcana]] DC 38 (master) to suppress the psychic teleportation field for 10 minutes.  **Critical Failure:** The trap immediately activates.

- **AC** 42, **Fort** +28, **Ref** +25
- **Hardness** 20, **HP** 80 (BT 40)
- **Weaknesses** physical 15 (except adamantine)

```ad-embed-ability
title: **Far Transport Pulse** `pf2:r`

- **Trigger**: A creature other than the Crimson Seer or his chosen servants touches or manipulates the lever to open the secret door.

**Effect** The trap fires a pulse of crushing psychic force targetin all creatures in a 30-foot emanation (DC 37 Will) and opens a dimensional gate.

> [!success-degree] 
> - **Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
> - **Success** The creature resists the worst of the psychic pulse and takes 15 mental damage.
> - **Failure** The creature takes 30 mental damage and is teleported to the designated torture chamber.
> - **Critical Failure** As failure, but the creature also becomes [[conditions#Confused|Confused 1]] by disorienting psychic echoes.

```

**Reset** The trap resets automatically after 1 round.
